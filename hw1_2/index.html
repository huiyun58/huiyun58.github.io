Moment of Inertia Calculator

A simple calculator for you to derive object's moment of inertia more intuitively.

What's moment of inertia?

The moment of inertia of a rigid body is a tensor that determines the torque needed for a desired angular acceleration about a rotational axis. It depends on the body's mass distribution and the axis chosen, with larger moments requiring more torque to change the body's rotation.
